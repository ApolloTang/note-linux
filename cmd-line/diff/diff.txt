::: Diff files present in two different directories:

	$diff -bur folder1/ folder2/
	
	source: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/2019857/diff-files-present-in-two-different-directories
    
::: To get summary of new/missing files, and which files differ:

    diff -arq folder1 folder2
    
    source: http://stackoverflow.com/questions/1644593/unix-compare-two-folders-which-has-many-files-inside-contents
