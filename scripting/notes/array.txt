Defining array:

a=(1 2 a b c d)

To access element of array by index (zero based).

    $ echo ${a[0]}
    1

    $ echo ${a[1]}
    2

    Note that curly brackets are required
    to access elements of array

Accessing all element of array:

    $ echo ${a[@]}
    1 2 a b c d

Get the length of array:

    $ echo ${#a[@]}
    6

Get last elements:

    $ echo ${a[${#a[@]}-1]}
    d
